The research — why 5v5 builds better 7v7 players

More touches = faster development

Small-sided game research consistently shows that 5v5 players average significantly more touches per minute than in 7v7 — with some studies showing nearly 3× the decision-making opportunities.
More touches per session means faster technical development, full stop.

Skills that transfer directly to 7v7

5v5 forces constant 1v1 situations, short passing combinations, and quick positional decisions under pressure — exactly the foundation that makes kids effective in 7v7. Players who put in summer 5v5 reps adapt faster, play with more confidence, and hit the ground running in the fall.
